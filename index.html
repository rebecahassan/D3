Poverty and Obesitity in the US

Correlations Identified Between Poverty and Obesity

There is an observable correlation between poverty and obesity rates in the United States. Where lower levels of poverty exist (CO, HI, MA), obesity is also low. The same is true for states with high level of poverty (MS, AR, WV), with a high percentage of the population suffers from obesity. There are indeed other factors that influence obesity in a state and need to be studied. Examples of the outliers are DC (high poverty, low obesity) and NM (high poverty, mid-level obesity).

PS: Notice that the x and y-axes have been modified to adjust for a high starting range for both poverty and obesity rates.
